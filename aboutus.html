Systematic Industrial Contractor (SMIC) is a Saudi National Company based in Al Khobar, KSA since 2012 as an Inspection and Contracting Company for Lifting Equipment Inspection, NDT, Drops and Rope Access Services to a wide range of Oil & Gas and Industrial Projects Our crane and lifting experts are conducting Onshore & Offshore crane inspection in accordance to ASME and other relevant international standards.

The service extends to all lifting equipment associated with Onshore & Offshore work-scopes from the supply of a shackle or rigging loft hire through to the complex installation of large pieces of equipment.

This insuring that our customers receive the best quality of equipment inspection & testing services dedicated to helping you achieve the certification you need.

We have the experience, knowledge and equipment to customize the absolute best possible solution for your individual situation which will help you reduce risk, shorten time to test the quality, safety and performance of your equipment against relevant health, safety and regulatory standards and to streamline your operation processes in a more sustainable manner.

We are one of the few lifting companies in Saudi Arabia offering training in Rigging & Slinging Inspection/ Operation and Fall Protection as well as Rescue training.
